Zig Zag Challenge

Using the penup, goto and forward commands, draw two horizontal lines that are 300 pixels long.

You may need to use goto to start at a specific location to allow the full length of the line.

The lines should be 75 pixels apart (vertically).

Using the left, right and forward commands, draw a zig zag line that creates 10 isosceles triangles.

Ensure that the tip of the triangle touches the upper line and the lower line forms the base.
