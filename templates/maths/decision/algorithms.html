1.5 Bin Packing Algorithms

LO:
  • To be able to carry out 3 bin packing algorithms and understand their strengths.
  • To be able to understand how to calculate the lower bound of the bins required.

Pre Lesson Videos

Using bullet points, describe in your exercise book how you complete the three bin filling algorithms.

Key Terms

The following terms are introduced: first-fit, first-fit decreasing, full-bin

Key Notes

  • Ensure that you are utilising the correct algorithm.
  • Ensure that you understand how to calculate the lower bound of the number of bins
  • Ensure that you understand the rules for calculating wasted space.
