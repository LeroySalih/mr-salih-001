Remember: Toolbox.

To solve an equation, you need to get the unknown (the variable) on its own.

General Rules.

  • Add to both sides
  • Subtract from both sides
  • Multiply both sides
  • Divide both sides
  • Square both sides
  • Square root

Remember: Steps

When solving an equation, always follow these steps.

  1. Simplify - If Possible.
  2. Solve - Get the Unknown Alone.
  3. Substitute - Check if your answer is correct.
