Remember: Multiplying Fractions

When we multiply fractions, we multiply the numerator and the denominator.

\(\frac{2}{3} \times x = \frac{2}{3} \times \frac{x}{1} = \frac{2x}{3}\)

So, when we see a fraction, such as \frac{4x}{6}, we can think of it as \frac{4}{6} \times x

Therfore,

\(\frac{4x}{6} = \frac{4}{6} \times x = \frac{2}{3} \times x = \frac{2x}{3}\)
