Venn Diagrams

  • Identify sections of venn diagram.
  • Identify regions based on Set notation.

Sections:

$\displaystyle \operatorname{vol}(A_1 \vee A_2) - \operatorname{vol}(A''_1 \vee A''_2) $ $\displaystyle = \binom{4x}{2x} - \binom{4x}{2x - 1} - \left(\binom{2x}{x}^2 - \binom{2x - 1}{x - 1}\binom{2x + 1}x\right) $

Finding Complex Regions

  • Shade Region A
  • Shade Region B
  • For A union B, find all regions that have been shaded once or more.
  • For A intersection B, find all regions that have been shaded twice.
